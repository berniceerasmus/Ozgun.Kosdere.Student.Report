FROM: director@eti.com.tr  |  TO: investment_team@eti.com.tr  |  SUBJECT: Q2 Procurement Policy Update
Dear team,

I am writing to clarify our Q2 procurement policy following recent budget overruns.

Mandatory requirements:
All purchase requests over 500,000 TL must be submitted with three competitive quotes. Engineers have to complete the procurement form before any order is placed — no exceptions.

Recommendations:
You should always keep a record of supplier communication in writing. Where possible, you could negotiate extended payment terms to protect cash flow.

Restrictions:
You must not approve any purchase without my written authorisation if the amount exceeds 1,000,000 TL. Engineers don't have to copy me on routine supplier emails — only escalate if there is a problem.

Note:
Delivery dates might shift in Q2 due to global logistics pressures. Please plan accordingly.

Best regards,
Director
