Part 2 — Best Option Multiple Choice

Select the connector that best matches the meaning.

10 MINAUTO-CHECK
▼
1) You want to show contrast inside one sentence.
Which connector fits best?
Correct — “although” joins two contrasting ideas in one sentence.
2) You want a formal result connector for a report.
Choose the best answer.
Exactly — “therefore” sounds logical and formal.
3) You need a connector before a noun phrase: “_____ supplier issues, delivery was late.”
Which one is correct?
Yes — “due to” is followed by a noun phrase.
4) Which connector means “if not”?
Choose carefully.
Correct — “unless” means “if not”.
