Choose the sentence that sounds professional, calm and respectful.

10 MINAUTO-CHECK
▼
1) You need a colleague to send a report today.
Choose the best professional request.
Correct — "Could you...?" is clear, polite and professional.
2) You notice a mistake in the data.
Choose the softest accurate sentence.
Exactly — the sentence focuses on the issue, not the person.
3) You disagree with a suggestion in a meeting.
Choose the most diplomatic response.
Correct — it acknowledges the other person first, then introduces another view.

Part 4 — B1 to B2 Tone Upgrade

Change direct workplace English into polished B2 professional English.

10 MINOPEN PRACTICE
▼
Upgrade each sentence. Use softeners such as: could, would, might, may, perhaps, I was wondering if, there seems to be, I am slightly concerned.
upgrade 01
B1: "I need this by Friday." → Write a polite professional request.
upgrade 02
B1: "The plan is not good." → Write a diplomatic comment about the plan.
upgrade 03
B1: "We have a problem with the supplier." → Write a softer update.
upgrade 04
B1: "You forgot to send the file." → Write a polite follow-up.
upgrade 05
B1: "I disagree." → Write a professional disagreement.
