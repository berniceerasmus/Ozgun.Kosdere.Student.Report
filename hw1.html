Part 4 — Tricky Multiple Choice

All options look correct — choose the one that best fits the context

8 MINHARD
▼
All three options may be grammatically possible. Choose the most appropriate one based on the context and meaning of the full sentence.
1. "I ______ in maintenance for nine years before this promotion. Now I am a unit manager."
→ Which tense is correct for an action that finished before the present role began?
2. "______ you ever managed an international team before this role?"
→ Asking about experience at any point in someone's life
3. "The Romania project ______ . We cannot change the scope now."
→ Something that happened recently and affects the present situation
4. A colleague asks: "When did you join ETİ?" You answer: "I ______ ETİ in 2015."
→ A specific date was given in the question
5. In a Monday meeting: "Team, I ______ all 40 project files over the weekend. Let me share what I found."
→ Recent action, result (the findings) matters now
6. "My director ______ me to explain the budget delay. I have a meeting in one hour."
→ Regular, recurring situation — part of the job
